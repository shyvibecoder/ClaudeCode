# Red-Team: Robotics, Space, Defense

**Verdict:** Real physical chokepoints, but investable expressions are already-priced darlings, bankrupt/impaired, private, or foreign. Retail/ETF investor structurally stuck buying the diluted proxy at the top.

## 1. ALREADY-PRICED
- RKLB: $3.47 (Apr-24) → ~$135 (May-26) = ~39x; analyst avg PT $103.91 (−28%). Trades as SpaceX-IPO proxy (FY25 rev $601M). SpaceX IPO mark cut below $2T de-rated the basket.
- MP: +365% YTD by Aug-25, ATH ~$82; priced for 10x magnet expansion not done until 2028; mostly LIGHT NdPr, doesn't solve Dy/Tb.
- VRT: +252%(2023)/+137%(2024)/+43%(2025); fwd P/E ~50–52, ~120% above fair value. CDU thesis = consensus AI trade, fully reflected.
- ASTS: ~210x fwd sales; Scotiabank PT $41 (−52%); needs 60 sats 2026 on heavy burn.
- WOLF: Chapter 11 (Jun-30-2025), emerged Sep-2025, legacy equity ~wiped (~95%+), delisting risk.
- Joby: cert slipped 2024→"late 2026", independent est mid-late 2027; pre-revenue.

## 2. DEMOS vs DEPLOYMENT
- Humanoid unit counts fiction: Musk "5,000 Optimus 2025" → reality a few hundred, supervised/R&D. Chokepoint demand doesn't exist at scale yet.
- eVTOL: no US type cert as of Q1-26; Joby TIA not started.
- Orbital DCs a pitch deck: Starcloud flew ONE H100; radiator mass ≈ compute mass (thermodynamic wall). "<15% by 2029-31" is generous.
- Golden Dome: $185B headline but CBO ~$1.2T/20yr; $0 contracts awarded as of Dec-25; only $13.4B appropriated; program lead doubts space interceptors.

## 3. SUBSTITUTION
- Reducers moat eroding NOW (present tense): Leaderdrive 2025 rev +47% to ¥570.7M, net profit >2x. Margin-compression for Harmonic/Nabtesco, not scarcity-rent.
- RE-free/reduced-Dy motors: Tesla pursuing rare-earth-free drive unit; if 30-50% of joints go RE-light, Dy/Tb scarcity halves. MP doesn't even make heavy RE at scale.
- Starlink D2C crushes ASTS TAM: >650 sats + EchoStar spectrum vs ASTS diluting for 60 BlueBirds.
- GaAs/GaN/SiC complementary not single chokepoint; krypton-vs-xenon routine substitution.

## 4. ACCESSIBILITY TRAP (killer for ETF investor)
- Private: SpaceX, Physical Intelligence, Skild, Scale AI (the actual demo→deployment data gate), Anduril ($61B).
- Foreign: Harmonic Drive/Nabtesco (Tokyo), Leaderdrive (Shenzhen), Schaeffler (Frankfurt).
- Impaired: Wolfspeed.
- Public/ETF = diluted proxy (KOID/BOTZ/ROBO/ARKX/UFO) loaded with megacap semis, not the chokepoint. Scarcity alpha captured by private/VC/foreign.

## 5. WOLFSPEED — load-bearing failure
GaN/GaAs "no RF substitute → investable via WOLF/NVTS" EMPIRICALLY FALSIFIED. WOLF held SiC/GaN substrate position and went bankrupt (capex intensity + Chinese SiC oversupply collapsing wafer prices + leverage). NVTS sub-scale, single-design-win story. RF GaN leaders diffuse (Qorvo/Macom/RTX/BAE/NXP). Treat WOLF/NVTS un-ownable for this thesis.

## MISSED (key principles)
- **CHOKEPOINT ≠ RENT.** Owning a scarcity ≠ pricing power. WOLF bankrupt holding chokepoint; Harmonic margin-compressed despite near-monopoly. The scarcity can be real and the equity still go to zero.
- **China oversupply = universal solvent** — broke SiC pricing (sank WOLF), breaking reducers, sets NdPr floor forcing DoD subsidy for MP.
- Power/substations/transformers (GEV) more structural 2026-28 throttle than GPUs/cooling.
- Concentration: RKLB/ASTS/VRT/MP move together as "hard-tech momentum" cohort — one correlated beta, de-rated jointly on SpaceX mark.
- No valuation/entry discipline — all narrative, no price-paid.
